A player is given a random number to match at the start of the game.
There are four crystals with varying values.
Each click will add the assigned value to a player's score.
A player's goal is to accumulate a total matching the random number shown without going over.
A player wins by matching his score to the number displayed.
The game is perpetually played after each round.
Click on the odd GEM to reset the games.
